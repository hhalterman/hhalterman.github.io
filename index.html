About Me

Want to know all about me? You're in the right spot. I'm Hailey, and I like to design and take photographs. I am creative and ambitious, ready to tackle anything life has to throw at me.

My creativity is what drove me to Bowling Green State University, and landed me into their Visual Communication Technology program, where I studied interactive media, photography, print media, and videography. On top of those subjects, I completed three internships and balanced my creative classes with marketing and business courses.

When I am not at work or school, I can be found adventuring with my camera by my side, since I most enjoy spending my time outdoors and with family. You also might find me hanging out in a hammock, curled up with a book and a coffee, kayaking like its my job, or cliff jumping.
